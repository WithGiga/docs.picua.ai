Welcome to PiCUA WebPanel! Let's get you up and running in just a few minutes.

## First Time Setup

### 1. Open PiCUA WebPanel

- Launch PiCUA from your applications menu
- Your browser will open automatically
- You'll see the PiCUA welcome screen [illustration]

### 2. Connect to a PiKVM Device

**If you have a PiKVM device:**

1. **Enter Device Details**:
   - IP Address: Your PiKVM device's IP (e.g., `192.168.1.100`)
   - Username: Your PiKVM username (usually `admin`)
   - Password: Your PiKVM password

2. **Click Connect**:
   - PiCUA will test the connection
   - You'll see a success message if everything works [illustration]



### 3. Explore the Interface

Once connected, you'll see:

- **Main Control Panel**: Keyboard, mouse, and power controls
- **Screen View**: Live view of the target computer
- **Status Bar**: Connection and device information
- **Menu**: Access to all PiCUA features [illustration]

## Your First Actions

### Try Basic Controls

1. **Move the Mouse**:
   - Click anywhere on the screen view
   - The mouse on the target computer will move [illustration]

2. **Type Text**:
   - Click the text input field
   - Type something and press Enter
   - Text appears on the target computer [illustration]

3. **Take a Screenshot**:
   - Click the camera button
   - Save the image to your computer [illustration]

### Test Power Control

1. **Check Power Status**:
   - Look at the power indicator
   - Green = On, Red = Off, Yellow = Unknown

2. **Try Power Commands**:
   - Click the power button to see options
   - **Don't actually turn off** unless you're sure!

## Understanding the Interface

### Main Areas

- **Top Bar**: Connection status and device info
- **Left Panel**: Control buttons and settings
- **Center**: Live screen view of target computer
- **Right Panel**: Additional tools and options [illustration]

### Control Buttons

- **Keyboard**: Text input and key commands
- **Mouse**: Movement and clicking controls
- **Power**: Turn computer on/off/restart
- **Screen**: Screenshots and screen analysis
- **Storage**: Virtual drive management

## Safety First

### Before You Start

- **Know Your Target**: Make sure you're controlling the right computer
- **Save Work**: Ensure important work is saved
- **Test Small**: Try small actions before big ones
- **Have a Plan**: Know what you want to accomplish

### Safe Practices

- **Start with Viewing**: Just look at the screen first
- **Use Demo Mode**: Practice without real hardware
- **Read Messages**: Pay attention to warnings
- **Ask for Help**: Don't guess if unsure

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues
- **Check IP Address**: Make sure it's correct
- **Verify Credentials**: Username and password
- **Network Access**: Can you reach the device?

### Control Problems
- **Mouse Not Moving**: Check if PiKVM is working
- **Keyboard Not Responding**: Try reconnecting
- **Screen Not Updating**: Refresh the connection
