PiCUA gives AI agents many ways to control physical computers. Here's everything it can do:

## Connection & Control

### Login & Setup
- **Connect**: AI agent connects to PiCUA with device details
- **Authenticate**: Secure login to the target computer
- **Status Check**: See if connection is working
- **Disconnect**: Safely end the session

### Power Management
- **Turn On**: Start the computer
- **Turn Off**: Shut down the computer
- **Reset**: Restart the computer
- **Power Cycle**: Turn off and on again

## AI Agent Capabilities

### What AI Can Do
- **See the Screen**: AI can see everything on the computer
- **Make Decisions**: AI decides what actions to take
- **Execute Commands**: AI sends commands through PiCUA
- **Get Feedback**: AI learns from what happens
- **Adapt**: AI changes approach based on results [illustration]

### Common AI Tasks
- **Login Automation**: AI can log into systems automatically
- **Form Filling**: AI fills out forms and applications
- **Error Recovery**: AI fixes problems when they happen
- **System Monitoring**: AI watches for issues
- **Data Entry**: AI enters information automatically [illustration]

## Real-World Examples

### Server Management

AI agent uses PiCUA to:

AI agent uses PiCUA to:

## How It All Works Together

1. **AI Agent** decides what needs to be done
2. **PiCUA Software** receives commands from AI
3. **Hardware** (like PiKVM) executes the commands
4. **Physical Computer** responds to the commands
5. **Feedback** goes back to AI through PiCUA
6. **AI Agent** makes decisions based on results [illustration]

## The Power of PiCUA

PiCUA turns AI agents from software-only tools into physical computer operators. Instead of just thinking and planning, AI can now:
- **See** what's happening on computers
- **Control** computers like humans do
- **Learn** from real-world interactions
- **Adapt** to changing situations
- **Work** 24/7 without breaks [illustration]

This creates a new kind of automation - where AI doesn't just process information, but actively controls and manages physical systems in the real world.
